Ingredients

  • 1 cup of white sugar
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 3tbsp cornstach
  • 11/2 cups water
  • 2 lemons, juice and zested
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 4 eeg yolks, beaten
  • 1 (9 inc) pie crust, baked
  • 4 egg whites

Directions

  1. Preheat oven:Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. Make lemon Filling:In a medium sourcepan...
    • Whisk together 1 cup sugar, flour,cornstach and salt
    • Stir water, lemon juice and lemon zest
    • Cook over medium high heat, stirring frequently untill mixture comes to a boil.
    • Stir in Butter
    • Place egg yolk in a small bowl and gradually wisk 1/2 a cup of hot sugar mixture.
    • Wisk eeg yolk mixture back into remaining sugar mixture.
      • Bring to cook while stiring contsantly untill thick.
    • Remove from heat
    • Pour filling into baked patry shell.
  3. Make Meringue: In a large glass of metal bowl....
    • White eeg whites untill foamy.
    • Add sugar gradually untill stiff peaks form.
    • spread meringue over pie, sealling the edges at the crust
  4. Bake: Bake in a preheated oven for 10 minutes, or untill meringue is golden brown.
